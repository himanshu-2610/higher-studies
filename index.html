Our Objectives

The Higher Education and Competitive Exam Cell (विजिगीषा) is committed to guiding students towards academic excellence and professional success through comprehensive support and resources.

01

Guidance & Mentorship

To guide and mentor students aspiring for higher education in India and abroad with personalized counseling sessions and roadmap planning.

02

Exam Preparation Support

To provide structured preparation support for competitive examinations such as GATE, GRE, TOEFL, IELTS, CAT, UPSC, MPSC, and other relevant exams.

03

Workshops & Seminars

To organize seminars, expert sessions, workshops, and counselling programs related to postgraduate studies, scholarships, fellowships, and academic pathways.

04

University Collaborations

To facilitate collaboration with universities, training organizations, and industry to enhance student opportunities for advanced studies and competitive readiness.

05

Student Team Building

To build and nurture a strong team of motivated students aspiring for higher studies through peer mentoring and collaborative learning groups.

06

Data Management

To maintain comprehensive data and documentation related to students' higher studies, exam performance, and progression for continuous improvement.

07

Student Motivation

To motivate and encourage students to pursue higher education through success stories, alumni interactions, and motivational sessions.

About PCCOE

Established in 1999, PCCOE has grown to become a center of excellence in engineering education.

We offer various undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and technology.

Academics

We offer B.Tech, M.Tech, and Ph.D. programs in various engineering disciplines.

Our curriculum is designed to meet industry standards and foster innovation.

Research & Development

PCCOE encourages research activities with well-equipped labs and funding opportunities.

Our faculty and students regularly publish papers in national and international journals.

Admissions

Admissions are based on merit through various entrance exams and direct admission processes.

Check our admission portal for current openings and eligibility criteria.

Placements

We have an excellent placement record with top companies visiting our campus every year.

Our training and placement cell provides comprehensive support to students.
